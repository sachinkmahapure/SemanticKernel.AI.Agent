# TechStore Product Knowledge Base

## Return Policy
All products can be returned within 30 days of purchase with original packaging.
Digital downloads and opened software are non-returnable.
Defective items qualify for immediate replacement or full refund.

## Warranty Information
- Laptops: 2-year manufacturer warranty + optional 1-year extension
- Peripherals (mice, keyboards, webcams): 1-year warranty
- Storage (SSDs, HDDs): 3-year limited warranty
- Memory (RAM): Lifetime limited warranty
